Core Values

Sign Sense Systems is a company dedicated to increasing the inclusivity of the deaf and hard-of-hearing community so they may participate meaningfully in any endeavor, whether that be work, school, or social activity. With the advanced technology readily available to us and the people with the right skill and dedication, there is no reason or excuse for any person to be left behind because of a disability.

We believe that accessibility is a fundamental human right for all. We are expanding communication possibilities and providing these services with dignity. We design our services with the deaf community in mind so that people who are deaf or hard of hearing may fully participate in their business seamlessly. We strive to help remove common stigmas from linguistic minorities. Our aim is to help spread awareness of the deaf community and their specific needs.

We strongly believe that diversity is an asset to our company because different people bring unique and valuable perspectives that are otherwise not attainable. Our team is proudly made up of diverse individuals from all different backgrounds. Our business can be an asset to you because we have such a wide range of perspectives to offer. Additionally, we have a zero-tolerance approach to discrimination which is reflected in our team, clienteles, and affiliated partners.
