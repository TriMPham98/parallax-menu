Respect for Individuals

Sign Sense Systems is committed to treating everyone with respect and dignity. We will not discriminate based on gender, age, race, ethnicity, religion, sexual orientation, or for any other personal characteristic. We will protect against bias in facial recognition with extensive testing on a large variety of test subjects. We will strive to protect our customers' privacy as well. Finally, we will be accountable for all software and will ensure that it works as intended and without bias wherever possible. While every technology is susceptible to bias, we will scrutinize our technology and do our best to avoid it.
