Carol Padden

Carol Padden is a professor at the University of California San Diego and an American Sign Language pioneer. She has studied the way that language influences our lives, particularly with American sign language, the way that language allows us to interact with the world. Dr. Padden will be incredibly helpful to us thanks to her experience in the practical use of sign language, and potential slang or expressions that we do not know because sign language is a somewhat rare language.
