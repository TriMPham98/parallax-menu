Effectiveness of Strategic Partnerships and Collaborations

To test accessibility, we can create a simple web-based demo of our technology using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. This demo can be easily shared with potential users, who can then provide feedback on the user interface, ease of use, and overall experience.

To test affordability, we can create various pricing models and present them to potential users through surveys or focus groups. Participants will be asked to evaluate the pricing options based on their perceived value and affordability.

Measurable outcomes:

1. The percentage of users who find the demo accessible and easy to use.

2. The preferred pricing model among potential users and the percentage of users who consider it affordable.
