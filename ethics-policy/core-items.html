1. Safety and Security: Our company understands that there are inherent risks in working with drones, and we strive to prioritize the safety of our customers, employees, and the general public in everything we do. We are constantly scrutinizing our technology to ensure accuracy and avoid bias in our products. We also focus heavily on the data security and safety of our customers, and we refuse to sell data to companies with detectable malicious intent. Finally, we will ensure that our technology is ethically sourced, and created in an environment that treats everyone fairly and equally.

2. Respect for Individuals: Sign Sense Systems is committed to treating everyone with respect and dignity. We will not discriminate based on gender, age, race, ethnicity, religion, sexual orientation, or for any other personal characteristic. We will protect against bias in facial recognition with extensive testing on a large variety of test subjects. We will strive to protect our customers' privacy as well. Finally, we will be accountable for all software and will ensure that it works as intended and without bias wherever possible. While every technology is susceptible to bias, we will scrutinize our technology and do our best to avoid it.

3. Responsibility and Accountability: Our company takes responsibility for the impact of our technology on society and the environment. We will ensure that our operations comply with all environmental laws and regulations, and we will take responsibility for any harm caused by our technology. We will also be transparent and accountable in our regulation and correction processes. Finally, we will acknowledge our mistakes and be open about our plans to correct them, what the mistake was, and how we can inform the future with our past mistakes.

4. Integrity and Honesty: Sign Sense Systems values integrity and honesty in all aspects of our operations. We will ensure that our technology is used for its intended purposes and prevent it from causing harm to anyone. We also will be vigilant in regards to bias inside our company, and ensure that we create an environment that encourages and welcomes people from all walks of life and interests. Furthermore, we will create an atmosphere of transparency for our employees, customers, and shareholders. We will discuss financial standing, current developments, and goals for the future with our people as frequently and openly as possible.

5. Continuous Improvement: Our company is committed to continuous improvement in every area. We will regularly review and update our policies and procedures to ensure that we are aligned with ethical principles and best practices in the industry. Finally, we promise to be open to new ideas and accept input from all voices surrounding our technology. We promise to engage with individuals who are hard of hearing and integrate their wishes into our software, to aid them as directly and consistently as possible.

This is our current set of ethical standards. Our set of ethical standards is subject to review and change as changes in technology and the scope of capabilities change, and we will forever strive for a better tomorrow.
