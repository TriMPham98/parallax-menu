Tri Pham - CEO

Tri Pham is a visionary entrepreneur and the CEO of Sign Sense Systems, a pioneering company that uses computer vision technology to develop sign language translators. He was born in Vietnam in 1998 and grew up fascinated by software, which instilled in him a strong interest in technology and innovation from a young age. In addition, Tri has extensive experience in teaching music, which requires a strong understanding of patterns and rhythms which is crucial in translating visual patterns in computer vision.
